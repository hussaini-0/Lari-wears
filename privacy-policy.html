PRIVACY POLICY

Your information is handled only for orders, support, and store operations.

Last updated: June 26, 2026

LARI respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share when placing an order, contacting the brand, or using this website.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we may collect your full name, email address, phone number, shipping address, payment method selection, and any payment proof or transaction reference you voluntarily provide for manual payments.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process orders, confirm payments, arrange delivery, provide customer support, respond to inquiries, and maintain internal order records. We do not collect more personal information than needed for these purposes.

3. Payment Information

LARI does not store card details on this website. If you use manual payment methods such as bank transfer, Easypaisa, or JazzCash, any payment screenshot or transaction ID you submit is used only to verify your order and complete fulfilment.

4. Storage and Security

Order and store-management data are stored using managed backend services selected by LARI. Product and media uploads may be stored through third-party cloud media services. Reasonable technical measures are used to limit unauthorized access, but no online system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Sharing of Information

Your information is shared only where necessary to run the store, such as hosting providers, database providers, media storage providers, or delivery and support operations. LARI does not sell customer personal information to third parties.

6. Retention

We keep customer and order information for as long as it is reasonably required to manage purchases, resolve support issues, maintain business records, and comply with applicable legal or operational requirements.

7. Cookies and Technical Data

This website may use basic browser storage and technical session data needed for shopping bag functionality, storefront behavior, security, and admin access. We do not promise a complex analytics or advertising profile on this site unless separately disclosed in future updates.

8. Your Rights

You may request correction or deletion of your personal information, subject to any recordkeeping or operational obligations that require us to retain certain order details. To make a privacy-related request, contact LARI through the contact page or official support channel.

9. Updates to This Policy

LARI may update this Privacy Policy when business operations, legal requirements, payment methods, or technology providers change. The latest version posted on this page will apply.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is used, please contact LARI through the contact page.
