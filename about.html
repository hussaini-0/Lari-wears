ABOUT LARI

LARI is a fashion brand born from creativity, passion, and purpose.

Founded by Laiba Lari, the journey of LARI began as a textile thesis project, where design became a medium for storytelling and self-expression. What started as an academic vision soon evolved into a growing women's fashion brand dedicated to empowering women through meaningful fashion.

Guided by its signature tagline, Wear Your Story, LARI believes that every garment carries a narrative. Each collection is thoughtfully designed to reflect confidence, resilience, independence, and individuality, allowing women to express their unique identities through style.

As the brand grew, it transformed from a thesis concept into a fashion label, bringing Laiba Lari's creative vision to life and connecting with a community that values authenticity and self-expression. Today, LARI continues to create designs that are more than clothing. They are stories waiting to be worn.
